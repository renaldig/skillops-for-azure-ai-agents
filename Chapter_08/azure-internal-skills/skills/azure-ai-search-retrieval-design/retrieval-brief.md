Application: HR policy assistant
Users: Employees asking questions about leave, benefits, and onboarding.
Source documents: PDF policy files and SharePoint pages.
Known issue: Some policy pages are outdated.
Need: Answers should cite the document or page used.
Access rule: The assistant must not answer questions about individual employee records.
Sample questions:
- How many annual leave days do I have?
- What happens if I am sick during annual leave?
- How do I request parental leave?
- Can you show my manager's approval history?
