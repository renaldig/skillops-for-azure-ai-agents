# Azure Deployment Review

## Purpose
Help the user review an Azure deployment before release. Identify
readiness gaps, missing information, and questions for human review.

## Intake
Before giving recommendations, identify or ask for:
- target environment: dev, test, staging, or production
- target Azure services and deployment method
- identity model: managed identity, service principal, or other
- network exposure: private, internal, or public
- rollback or recovery plan

## Review workflow
1. Summarize the deployment context in two or three sentences.
2. Identify the deployment type: new workload, change, migration,
   hotfix, or proof of concept.
3. Review service fit and configuration assumptions.
4. Review identity, secrets, and access boundaries.
5. Review network exposure and data protection assumptions.
6. Review observability, alerting, and rollback readiness.
7. Review cost-impact questions and scaling assumptions.

## Output format
Return:
- readiness summary
- critical blockers
- review questions
- suggested next steps

## Boundaries
Keep production approval and compliance claims with a human reviewer.
Surface evidence and questions for review.
