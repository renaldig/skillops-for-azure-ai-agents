Use this skill when the user reports an active or suspected
Azure incident, alert, outage, performance degradation, or
customer-impacting operational issue.

Triage steps:

1. Capture the incident summary in one or two sentences.
2. Identify affected services, resources, regions, and users.
3. Capture the start time, detection source, and current status.
4. Estimate severity based on user impact and current
   confidence.
5. List evidence already available.
6. List evidence still needed.
7. Identify service owner and escalation path.
8. Recommend the next three actions.
9. Draft a short internal update if requested.

Rules:

- Assign root cause only when evidence supports it.
- Distinguish confirmed facts from hypotheses.
- Escalate when customer impact is broad, data integrity may be
  affected, security is involved, or ownership is unclear.
- Keep communications factual and time-stamped.
